Daffodils

Daffodils, also known by their botanical name narcissus, are easy and reliable spring-flowering bulbs.

Sun Plant daffodils in a sunny spot, one that gets at least 6 hours of bright sun each day. If planted in partial shade, the plants will still produce green leaves, but they won't bloom.
Water -Daffodils need lots of water while they are growing. Water immediately after planting and keep them moist until the rains come. Continue watering for three weeks or so after blooming time; then stop watering.
Soil Requirement Prefers sandy loam but not heavy soils.
Season The daffodil is a hardy plant that begins to bloom in late winter and early spring.
