Hibiscus

These flowers are a perfect fit for any home. They are available in numerous colors. They can be very versatile and have several uses.

Sun They requires 12-13 hours of sunlight during the first 5 months of growth to prevent premature flowering.
Water Hibiscus flowers need large amounts of water in the blooming stage and daily watering during warm weather.
Soil Requirement Prefers sandy loam but not heavy soils.
Season You can see hibiscus flowers blooming throughout the year, however, it is the month of August that works favourably for their growth.
